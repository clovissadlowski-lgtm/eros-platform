# Higeia — Product Metrics and Success Criteria

## North Star candidate

Number of professionally reviewed patient follow-up cycles completed with useful structured information.

## Activation

- Professional invited first patient.
- Patient completed anamnesis.
- First check-in completed.
- First summary reviewed.

## Engagement

- Weekly active professionals.
- Weekly active patients.
- Check-in completion rate.
- Summary review rate.

## Value

- Time saved per patient.
- Perceived usefulness.
- Reduction in manual consolidation.
- Professional retention intent.

## Quality

- Summary correction rate.
- Omission rate.
- Incorrect statement rate.
- Acceptance rate.

## Safety

- Unsafe output rate.
- Escalation rate.
- Incident count.
- Dispute rate.

## Cost

- Cost per summary.
- Cost per active patient.
- Cost per professional.
- Human review cost.

## Alpha success proposal

- End-to-end flow works.
- No unresolved critical incident.
- Professional usefulness demonstrated.
- Quality is measurable.
- Cost is understood.
- Stop criteria remain untriggered.
