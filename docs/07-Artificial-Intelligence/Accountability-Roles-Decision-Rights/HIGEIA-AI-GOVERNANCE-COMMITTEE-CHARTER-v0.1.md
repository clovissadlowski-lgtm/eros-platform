# Higeia — AI Governance Committee Charter

## Purpose

Revisar decisões críticas, incidentes, exceções, risco residual e evolução da governança de IA.

## Members

- Founder
- Product
- AI
- Engineering
- Clinical
- Safety
- Privacy
- Security
- Operations

## Responsibilities

- Approve critical changes.
- Resolve conflicts.
- Review major incidents.
- Review open critical risks.
- Approve exceptions.
- Review model and vendor changes.
- Review deprecations.
- Track governance actions.

## Meetings

- Operational review: weekly.
- Governance review: monthly.
- Strategic review: quarterly.

## Quorum

To be defined.

## Records

Every meeting must record:

- attendees;
- agenda;
- decisions;
- blockers;
- owners;
- due dates;
- dissent;
- next review.
