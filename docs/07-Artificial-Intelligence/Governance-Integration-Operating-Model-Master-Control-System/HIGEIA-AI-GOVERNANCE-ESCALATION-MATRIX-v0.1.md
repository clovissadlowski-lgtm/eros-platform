# Higeia — AI Governance Escalation Matrix

**Versão:** 0.1

| Trigger | Initial Owner | Escalate To | Maximum Response Time | Required Record | Immediate Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Critical safety risk | Safety Owner | Founder/Clinical Authority | Immediate | Incident/Decision | Restrict or stop |
| Personal data incident | Privacy/Security | Founder/Legal | Immediate | Incident | Contain and assess |
| Critical control failure | Control Owner | Risk/Founder | Immediate | Finding/Exception | Compensating control |
| Severe cost anomaly | FinOps/Operations | Founder | Same day | Anomaly record | Limit usage |
| Vendor outage | Operations | Engineering/Vendor Owner | Per service RTO | Incident | Activate fallback |
| Expired critical exception | Governance Owner | Founder | Immediate | Exception record | Block affected activity |

## Regra

Escalonamento não substitui contenção imediata quando houver risco.
